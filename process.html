Our Process

From Idea to Launch

Every project follows our battle-tested workflow designed for predictable results and clear communication at every step.

1

Discovery & Planning

We start by deeply understanding your vision, target audience, and business goals. Our team conducts market research, defines scope, and creates a detailed project roadmap with clear milestones.

Project Brief Technical Spec Cost Estimate Timeline Risk Assessment
2

Prototyping & Design

We create rapid prototypes to validate core mechanics and gameplay loops. Simultaneously, our art team develops the visual direction — concept art, UI wireframes, and style guides.

Playable Prototype Concept Art UI/UX Wireframes Style Guide GDD
3

Production & Development

The main development phase using agile sprints. Regular demos, code reviews, and milestone check-ins ensure we stay perfectly aligned with your vision. We build in 2-week sprint cycles.

Sprint Demos Build Artifacts Progress Reports Code Repository Asset Pipeline
4

Quality Assurance & Testing

Comprehensive testing at every level — functional, performance, compatibility, and user acceptance testing. Our QA team runs automated and manual test suites to ensure a polished final product.

Test Plans Bug Reports Performance Metrics Compatibility Matrix UAT Sign-off
5

Launch & Support

We handle deployment, store submissions, and launch-day monitoring. Post-launch, we provide ongoing support, content updates, and live operations to keep your game thriving.

Store Submissions Launch Monitoring Hotfix Support Analytics Setup Content Updates
