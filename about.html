Our Mission

Building Games That Matter

SciMyth Games was founded with a singular vision: to create a world-class game development studio that combines artistic excellence with technical mastery. We believe every game has the power to transport players to new worlds and tell stories that resonate.

From our roots in indie development to our current position as a trusted outsourcing partner for studios worldwide, we've never lost the passion that drives us — the love of building incredible games.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

1

2009 — The Beginning

Founded as a small indie studio by a group of passionate gamers and developers, SciMyth Games started with game jam entries and passion projects.

2

2014 — First AAA Partnership

Landed our first co-development contract with a major publisher, providing art and QA services for a AAA action title. Team grew to 20 members.

3

2018 — Global Expansion

Opened remote offices across 3 continents, expanded into full-cycle development, and doubled our team to 40+ specialists.

4

2024 — 60+ and Growing

Today, SciMyth Games is a 60+ member studio serving clients in 12 countries, with expertise spanning Unity, Unreal Engine 5, and custom engine development.
